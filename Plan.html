04

Three homepage directions

三个方向

All share the palette & type above — they differ in layout and mood. Next step is to build these three as real screens so you can compare. Pick one (or mix).

Direction A

Heritage Apothecary

悬壶

Photo-led and editorial. A full-bleed warm image of the treatment room or dried herbs fills the hero, with a serif headline and the phone number overlaid. The red seal stamp sits in a corner like a signature. Vertical Chinese type accents and a quiet, authoritative tone. This leans hardest into trust & lineage — it feels like a long-established practice.

Photo heroEditorialSeal motifMost traditional
Direction B

Botanical Calm

本草

Airy and serene, spa-like. Lots of cream whitespace, a centered serif headline, delicate botanical line motifs, and soft sage cards for each service. Minimal imagery, maximum calm — it lowers the anxiety of someone in pain or seeking help. The gentlest, most welcoming of the three.

WhitespaceCenteredSereneSoft cards
Direction C

Modern Heritage

脉

A structured editorial grid with strong typographic hierarchy and an asymmetric layout. The long list of treatable conditions becomes an elegant index — turning your breadth of expertise into a confident showpiece. Respects tradition but feels contemporary and designed, the most distinctive of the three.
